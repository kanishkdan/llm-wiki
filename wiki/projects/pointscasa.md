# PointsCasa

PointsCasa is a flight award search engine and Kanishk's primary project. It represents the realization of building a technical product "from scratch" to solve a personal pain point in the loyalty travel space.

## Development
- **Backend:** Python/Django with Celery for background tasks.
- **Infrastructure:** PostgreSQL and Playwright for airline scrapers.
- **Growth:** Focused on iterating based on user feedback and sales realizations.

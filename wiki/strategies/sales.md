# Sales Strategy

Realisations on selling products based on Kanishk's experience.

## The Core Realisation
Sales is more about being able to find the right people to sell to than actual selling skills. The more "salesy" you get on a call, the more likely you are to miss the sale.

## Principles of Selling
- **Unlock value quickly:** Maintain scripts and pitches, but cut down the time it takes to show value as much as possible.
- **Radical Honesty:** Be upfront when the product is not a fit.
- **Second and Third Level Use Cases:** Spend time charting these out; a user's reason for buying might be a use case you never initially considered.
- **Generous Free Trials:** If fit is uncertain, get the product in their hands immediately.
- **Feedback Loops:** Every user interaction is an opportunity for ideas and feedback straight from potential long-term customers.

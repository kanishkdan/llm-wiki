# Digital Operator

A "Digital Operator" is a specialized AI agent designed for full-stack execution rather than simple conversation. Unlike standard chatbots, a digital operator possesses a shell, a browser, and direct access to the user's workspace.

## Characteristics
- **Resource for execution:** Capable of running scripts, managing files, and triggering builds.
- **Pragmatic:** Focused on shipping and "getting the job done" (e.g., Rudra managing the kanishkdan.com blog).
- **Proximity to data:** Lives where the work happens to maximize utility.
